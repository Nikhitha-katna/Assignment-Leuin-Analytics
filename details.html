Overview of Mobility Apps

Ola

Ola is an Indian-based ride-hailing service founded in 2010 in Mumbai. It offers a variety of services including cab rides, auto-rickshaws, bike taxis, and even electric vehicles. Ola is one of the largest ride-hailing companies in India and has expanded its operations internationally to countries such as Australia and the United Kingdom. Ola’s goal is to make transportation more affordable, efficient, and accessible through technology-driven solutions.

Uber

Uber is a global leader in ride-hailing services, operating in over 900 metropolitan areas worldwide. Founded in 2009, Uber revolutionized the transportation industry by providing convenient and affordable rides through its app. Uber offers a range of services including UberX (standard rides), UberPOOL (carpooling), and Uber Eats (food delivery).
Services: Ride-hailing, food delivery, freight, micro-mobility (scooters/bikes).

Rapido

Rapido is an Indian bike taxi service that helps commuters beat traffic by offering affordable bike rides across cities. Founded in 2015, Rapido is known for providing quick and affordable rides with a fleet of registered drivers. The app allows users to book a bike ride, reducing the time and cost associated with traditional taxi services.
Services: Bike taxis, auto rickshaw services.
